MARKETING

Marketing

Context of campaign and promotional activities

Hayy’s has conducted 4 instagram campaigns

The Dare campaign

The campaign is about inspiring people to dare. Hayy challenges the audience to step up to new people, start the conversation. This campaign is reinforced with the #getsocial and #starttheconversation

The Hayy beers introduction campaign

In this campaign we introduced our 3 types of craft beer to our followers. Presenting first the Hayy original beer. Then we introduced the IpHayy (IPA) and last but not least, the Hayy Hayy Hayy (Tripel) beer. With these 3 posts our audience discovered the Hayy bottle designs.

The response campaign

This campaign was hold in Hayy’s Instagram stories. Thanks to this campaign we were able to measure the response from our audience regarding our brand message. The followers had to respond to 3 polls.
1. Did you meet a new person this week? 83% YES/ 17% NO from the 12 votes
2. Did you start the conversation? 55% YES/ 45% NO from the 11 votes
3. How confident are you to start a conversation with a stranger? (Gradation answer tool on insta stories) The average felt pretty confident to start a conversation with a stranger.

The become part of the community campaign

Hayy is part of the special beer night event in Breda. Hayy promoted this event on the Instagram and inspired the followers to join the event and become part of the Hayy family.

Learning Points

During the project we could have organised our media campaigns in a more stuctured way. We started posting halfway of the project because we weren’t done with the final logo yet. If we would have been earlier done with the final brand colors/finished logo, the response on our campaigns could have been beter. We learned that our brand message was hard to measure because it is about people daring to be social with new people. We can only rely on honest answers from our followers on polls. But we can't measure if our target audience acted upon our brand message. We also learned that we could have done more regarding gaining more followers that are not other Brand Development accounts or second year Creative Business students.
On the other hand we were suprised by the many positive responses from our followers on our branding, people liked the designs and inside jokes from our beer bottles. People also liked the campaigns, they found them interactive and genuine.

Future Planning

Regarding the planning of our social media posts we believe we could have been more organized and plan better ahead instead of gowing with the flow. If the project would continue we would definetly do more research for new campaigns and make a clearer content planning.

Future recommandations would be that we would love to elaborate our dare campaign to inspire people with our brand message, we would enjoy doing more campaigns linked to events where Hayy beer would be available because we think that our social media should be only a place where we inform our target audience about our brand, but also a place where we motivate them to gather and be social in real life.

Professionalism

Present and organize below all social media/online activities of the campaign. Context and material are according to the brand style, image, and vision. Images on the site are of high-quality, readable, and properly designed.
